A Tribute to Prof. R.K. Singh: A Legacy of Leadership and Vision

By IGDTUW Community ‚Ä¢ September 2025

"A teacher affects eternity; he can never tell where his influence stops." ‚Äì Henry Adams

A Heartfelt Farewell

As R.K. Singh Sir's tenure as Dean Student Welfare comes to an end, we would like to present our heartfelt gratitude for his tireless efforts. During the past three years, sir has shown exemplary support to the student body. His commendable vision of providing ample scope, opportunities and facilities for multidimensional development of personality and leadership qualities among students is highly appreciated.

Transforming Campus Life

The increase in student engagement at socio-cultural activities and the enrichment of campus life is largely due to the endeavours of R.K. Singh Sir. Working alongside Prof. R.K. Singh has been an experience that taught us lessons far beyond the classroom. He is a pillar of vision and leadership, whose guidance has continually inspired the student community.

Building Bridges Through UCC

By founding the University Cultural Committee (UCC) in 2025, he has helped bridge the gap between students and faculty. He has demonstrated integrity and responsibility while managing multiple roles simultaneously, and his work ethic continues to inspire us.

The Taarangana Legacy

His contribution during Taarangana 2025 is worthy of the highest regard. Organising Taarangana was a tremendous task. From handling vendors to managing team tasks, sir guided each student with utmost care. He consistently worked longer hours than the students, often beginning before we arrived and staying until every task was completed. His attention to detail and ability to pave the path for students to host a successful event is truly admirable.

On behalf of the entire student community, we thank Prof. R.K. Singh for being a guiding light, paving the way for many of us to make our university experience a memorable one. We are eternally grateful to him for the visible growth of campus life, and we look forward to continuing to learn from him in his role as professor while carrying forward the legacy he has created.
