About IGDTUW Insider

The heartbeat of campus journalism at IGDTUW.

About Us

IGDTUW Insider is a passion project born from the desire to connect our vibrant university community. We believe in the power of words and visuals to inspire, inform, and unite. Our team, comprised entirely of students, is dedicated to bringing you stories that matter—from campus news and events to the groundbreaking achievements of our peers and faculty. We're more than just a publication; we're a platform for expression, a source of truth, and a record of our shared journey at IGDTUW.

Our Mission

IGDTUW Insider is dedicated to serving as the voice of our university community. We strive to inform, engage, and connect students, faculty, and staff through accurate reporting, thoughtful analysis, and compelling storytelling.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a vibrant and inclusive platform that empowers diverse voices, fosters critical thinking, and celebrates the spirit of IGDTUW. We aim to be the most trusted source of news and a cornerstone of campus dialogue.

What We Will Do

  • Report on campus news and events
  • Highlight student and faculty achievements
  • Provide a platform for diverse voices and opinions
  • Foster dialogue about issues that matter to our community
  • Celebrate the rich culture and traditions of IGDTUW
